About Us

History of PT. MEGA GLOBAL ENERGY

PT. Mega Global Energy (MGE) was established in 2007 as a subsidiary of LX International Corporation, South Korea. MGE operates in the fields of coal mining and coal trading. Currently, MGE has mining operations in Kutai Kartanegara Regency, East Kalimantan.

Our Services

At PT. Mega Global Energy (MGE), we specialize in comprehensive coal mining and trading services. From exploration and extraction to processing and distribution, we deliver high-quality coal efficiently and sustainably. Our commitment to environmental stewardship and regulatory compliance ensures responsible operations. With advanced technologies and a skilled team, we provide exceptional value, supporting industry growth and community prosperity.
